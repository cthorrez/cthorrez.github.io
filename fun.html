Fun Stuff

Occasionally in my free time I work on small side projects where I can apply statistics, python, machine learning, or miscelaneous data science related things to explore a topic that I find personally interesting or amusing.

LeBron 27/7/7

I read on reddit that LeBron James had averaged 27 points, 7 rebounds, and 7 assists per game for 15 seasons but had never completed one game with exactly those statistics. I attempted to model his statistics and calcualte the probability of that never happening. Code available here, writeup webpage here

Esports Data Science

This project combined my interests in machine learning and professional League of Legends. I scraped data on over 15,000 professional League of Legends matches and trained models to predict the winners of future games. I made a series of statistical posts on reddit and made predictions on twitter. This project is mostly inactive but I hope to return to modeling team sports and esports in the future.
